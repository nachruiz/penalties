How to play

Touch the ball on the spot you want to strike (top, bottom, sides).

Drag back like a slingshot. The triangle shows direction; longer drag = more power.

Release to shoot. Striking the side of the ball aims wider but risks going out.

Shot types (right column)

INSTEP: straight shot, default.

CURL: curves toward the strike side.

PANENKA: chip shot, slow and high. Goes out if too powerful.

PARADINHA: keeper commits before you shoot. Aim opposite his dive.
